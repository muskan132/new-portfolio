AnalyticsProductJan–Mar 2024 · MBA

Analyzing Consumer Behavior in Online Car Purchases

I led a market-research study into how people decide when buying cars online, analyzing 10,000+ data points and 50+ customer responses. Using regression, ANOVA, factor analysis, and cluster analysis in SPSS across 22 hypotheses, I found that brand, mileage, and functionality weigh almost equally — that virtual advisors lift conversions by 23%, and that test drives stay pivotal. I turned it into a 360° set of recommendations for optimizing an online car-buying platform.
